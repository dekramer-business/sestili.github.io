Family Owned and Operated, Since 1947

Located in South Oakland in Pittsburgh since it’s humble beginning. Originally founded as a landscaping service, Italian immigrants Max and Esther Sestili, operated the business from their home. The Sestili Nursery building was built in 1959, and is in its original place today.
